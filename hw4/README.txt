Instructions
------------
  1. Open your Google Drive in the browser.
  2. Create a directory called "DL4CV" in your Google Drive.
  3. In "DL4CV", create a sub-directory called "hw4".
  4. Upload ALL the files listed above to "hw4" under "DL4CV".
  5. Double-click on "ViT.ipynb" to open it in Google Colab.
  6. Follow the instructions in the notebook and solve the coding assignment.
  7. Double-click on "DCGAN/main.ipynb" to open it in Google Colab.
  8. Follow the instructions in the notebook and solve the GAN coding assignment.
  9. Run the last cell in the notebook to create a submission zip file and download it.
  10. Upload your submission zip to the course moodle.
